INFRATEC Management Review Process:
Inputs include: audit results, customer feedback/complaints, process performance & objectives, NCR/CAPA trends, risks & opportunities, resource adequacy, changes affecting the QMS, improvement opportunities.
Activities: review inputs, evaluate QMS suitability/adequacy/effectiveness, decide actions.
Outputs: actions for improvement, changes to QMS, resource needs, updated objectives.
Records: meeting agenda, minutes, action log with owners and due dates.
Held at planned intervals and whenever significant changes occur.
