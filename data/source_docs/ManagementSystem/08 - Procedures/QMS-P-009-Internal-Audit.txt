INFRATEC Internal Audit Process:
- Plan: Risk-based annual programme covering all processes and sites.
- Do: Competent, independent auditors; use checklists; record evidence & NCRs.
- Check: Issue audit reports within 5 working days with grades & actions.
- Act: Raise CAPAs; verify effectiveness; close on time.
- Records: audit plan, checklists, reports, NCR log, CAPAs, effectiveness reviews.
