INFRATEC Internal Audit Process:
- Plan: Risk-based annual audit programme covering all processes and sites.
- Do: Competent, independent auditors; use checklists; record evidence and nonconformities.
- Check: Issue audit reports within 5 working days with grades and recommendations.
- Act: Raise CAPAs; verify effectiveness; close within target timescales.
- Records: Audit plan/schedule, checklists, reports, nonconformity logs, CAPAs, effectiveness reviews.
